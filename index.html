Intro

I'm an aspiring front end developer, who loves good books, Doctor Who, coffee, and learning new things. I'm finishing up my associate's degree at Columbus State Community College, and am planning on graduating in May of 2015.

Where I'm From

I'm from a little (well, not so little anymore) town called Pataskala, Ohio. Growing up, it was a small town, where everyone knew everyone. In fact, my high school graduating class had 70 people in it. It's since grown quite a bit, and there's always something new popping up.

More About Me

What's important to you?

In Judaism, there's something called Tikkun Olam. It's Judaism envisioning an ideal world, and is often translated to mean "repair of the world", and even as social justice. The idea of repairing the world, of leaving things better than how we found them, is an important idea to me, and is one of the things that drew me to study Judaism.
